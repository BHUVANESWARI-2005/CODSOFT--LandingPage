Our Services

GELATO

Gelato is an Italian-style ice cream known for its rich, dense texture and intense flavors, made with a higher proportion of milk to cream. It is traditional ice cream, resulting in a creamier and more luxurious dessert experience.

GULFI

This traditional Indian ice cream is made by slowly heating sweetened milk until the sugar caramelizes and the milk condenses; the mixture is then frozen in cone-shaped molds without first being whipped or churned

SHERBET

Sherbet is a frozen dessert with a lower milk content than traditional ice cream, typically made with fruit juice or puree. It offers a refreshing and fruity alternative, perfect for those seeking a lighter, dairy-free option.

BUY NOW

FROZEN YOGURT

Frozen yogurt, or "froyo," a dessert incorporating yogurt cultures, delights with its tangy taste, smoother texture than traditional ice cream, and is often favored for its perceived healthiness,enjoyed adorned with toppings.

SOFT SERVE

Soft serve ice cream is a smoother, airier version of traditional ice cream, served directly from a machine without hardening in a freezer. Known for its creamy texture, it's a popular treat enjoyed in cones or cups with variety of toppings.

BUY NOW

PHILADELPHIA ICE CREAM

Philadelphia-style ice cream, skips the traditional custard base and is made with a mix of cream, milk, sugar, and flavorings, resulting in lighter, pure taste. It's characterized by consistency and allows the flavors to shine without the eggs.

BUY NOW

SNOW ICE

Snow ice cream, a unique frozen dessert, is crafted by shaving thin layers of ice and then drizzling them with sweetened condensed milk or flavored syrups, creating a velvety, snow-like texture that melts, offering a delightful frozen treat experience

ITALIAN ICE

Italian ice cream, commonly known as "gelato," stands out for its dense, creamy texture and intense flavors, achieved by using a higher proportion of milk to cream and churning it at a slower speed. It is a authentic frozen dessert experience.
